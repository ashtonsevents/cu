What is the Virtual Hub?

The Ashtons Virtual Hub supports primary care by addressing challenges in capacity, access, backlogs, new care models and workforce expansion.

Designed to deliver exceptional value and efficiency, it offers a wide range of services from clinical reviews and prescription management to appointment booking and medicines management reporting, all tailored to meet the transformation goals of practices and PCNs.
